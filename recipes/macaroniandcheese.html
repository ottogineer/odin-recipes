Macaroni and Cheese

Description

Cheesy and creamy elbow noodles

Ingredients

  • Box of Macaroni and Cheese
  • Water
  • 2 tbs butter
  • 1 cup milk
    •

    Steps

    1. Bring 1 quart of water to a boil in sauce pan
    2. Add macaroni noodles
    3. Cook for 10-15 minutes until noodles are soft
    4. Strain noodles and reduce heat to low
    5. Add noodles, butter, and milk to sauce pan, mix until creamy
    6. Turn off heat and let thicken for 5 minutes
    7. Serve
