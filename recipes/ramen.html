Ramen

Description

A deliciously seasoned and salty broth with tender noodles

Ingredients

  • Ramen packet
  • Water
    •

    Steps

    1. Bring 2 cups of water to a boil
    2. Add ramen noodles
    3. Cook for 5-10 minutes until noodles are soft
    4. Add seasoning packet and mix
    5. Serve
